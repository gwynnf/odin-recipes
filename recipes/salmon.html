Salmon

Decription

Though there are many salmon recipes on the internet. This is how my husband prepares the salmon for me. A recipe that utilizes a mixture of fresh lemon, lemon infused oil, and other spices or parsley garlic butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of salmon
  • 1 lemon
  • lemon infused olive oil or parsley garlic butter
  • 2 tsp of garlic powder
  • 2 tbp of black pepper
  • Optional: italian blend to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degree F
  2. Place salmon slices in glass casserole dish
  3. Optional: Pour oil over salmon slices
  4. Add garlic, pepper, and desired seasoning
  5. Optional: Place parsley garlic butter on top of salmon
  6. Add garlic, pepper, lemon seasoning, and italian blend to bowl
  7. Cook salmon for 20-30 minutes
  8. Optional: Squeeze lemon juice onto salmon
