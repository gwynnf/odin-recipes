Lemon Chicken

Decription

Though there are many lemon chicken recipes on the internet. This is my favorite lemon chicken recipe. A recipe that utilizes a mixture of fresh lemon, lemon infused oil, and other spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs of chicken
  • 1 lemon
  • lemon infused olive oil
  • 2 tsp of garlic powder
  • 2 tbp of black pepper
  • 1 Tbps of Penzi's lemon seasoning
  • italian blend to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degree F
  2. Slice chicken to desired size or keep whole
  3. Place chicken in bowl
  4. Coat infused oil over the chicken
  5. Add garlic, pepper, lemon seasoning, and italian blend to bowl
  6. Evenly coat chicken with seasoning
  7. Pour seasoned chicken into a casserole pan
  8. Spread the chicken evenly within the casserole dish
  9. Cook chicken for 20-30 minutes*
*Eating under cooked chicken can be hazardous to your health.
